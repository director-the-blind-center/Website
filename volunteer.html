Volunteer opportunities

Time commitments and openings should be confirmed with staff.

Programs and Lunches

Assist with classes, activities, lunches, and participant support.

Gift Shop and Crafts

Support shop tasks, crafters, open studios, and seasonal handmade items.

Events and Fundraisers

Help with setup, cleanup, guest support, vendor support, and fundraising activities.

Administration

Support office tasks, paperwork, calls, mailings, and community communication.

Gardening and Maintenance

Help with grounds, greenhouse tasks, handyman needs, and facility projects.

Recycling

Assist with recycling efforts listed on the old site, including cans, sneakers, and glass if currently active.

Application and background check

The previous volunteer form collected references, experience, preferences, transportation details, accommodation needs, and background check acknowledgment. The new site should use a shorter starter form first, with the full application as a second step.

Accommodation support

Volunteers can ask for help completing an application or request accommodations for serving at the Center.
