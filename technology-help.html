How the Center can help

Start with the task, not the device.

The Technology Discovery Lab can help you explore tools in plain language and decide what is worth practicing next. You can ask about phones, magnifiers, screen readers, artificial intelligence, smart-home tools, and adaptive devices.

  • Bring the device you already use, such as a phone, tablet, laptop, magnifier, or smart speaker.
  • Bring chargers, passwords you are comfortable using yourself, and one or two tasks you want to practice.
  • Ask about workshops or guided practice if you want repeated help learning a tool.
