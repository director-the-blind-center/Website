I need glasses

Get help finding usable eyewear.

The Community Eyewear Center helps people look for emergency replacement glasses, donated readers and sunglasses, free or subsidized eyeglasses, and community vision resources.

How this works

Start with what you need today.

Step 1

Tell Us What Happened

Call, email, or visit and explain whether glasses were lost, broken, no longer usable, or unaffordable.

Step 2

Bring Helpful Details

If you have them, bring your old glasses, current prescription, eye doctor information, insurance details, or a note about what is hard to see.

Step 3

Choose the Best Option

Staff can discuss donated inventory, emergency temporary eyewear, readers, sunglasses, Lions Club connections, or referral options.

Emergency replacement glasses

If glasses are broken, lost, or no longer usable, The Blind Center may be able to help locate a temporary pair from donated inventory. Inventory varies, and an exact prescription match cannot be guaranteed.

Emergency glasses are temporary and do not replace a current eye examination or professionally prescribed eyewear.

Give eyewear

Donate your used eyeglasses.

Subject to current policy, the Center may accept prescription eyeglasses, reading glasses, sunglasses, children's glasses, cases, selected accessories, and suitable safety glasses.
