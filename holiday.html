Holiday Market

Kris Kringle Holiday Village

A seasonal gathering with crafters, artisans, small business owners, hot cocoa, decorations, carolers, smores, vendors, volunteers, and community fun.

Ask About the Holiday Village

Current season details

Date, time, location, vendor fees, performer details, application deadline, and volunteer shifts should be added when the next Holiday Village is confirmed.

Accessibility note

Event listings should include parking, building access, service animal information, food notes, and an accommodation request contact.

Ways to participate

Attendees

Visit vendors, enjoy seasonal activities, and support the Center's mission.

Vendors

Crafters, artisans, and small business owners can request current application details.

Volunteers

Help with setup, cleanup, guest support, vendor support, and event flow.

Performers

Carolers and community performers can ask about available times and needs.
