Who We Help

Support for every stage of the vision journey.

The Blind Center serves people experiencing vision changes, people who are blind or have low vision, families, caregivers, professionals, donors, volunteers, and community partners.

People with vision changes

Learn next steps, practical tools, and ways to adapt without losing independence.

People who are blind or have low vision

Find community, technology, classes, resource navigation, and hands-on learning.

Children and families

Explore healthy vision habits, school success, eye safety, and family education.

Caregivers and supporters

Learn how to help without taking over, improve home safety, and reduce isolation.

Professionals and partners

Connect with referral resources, meeting space, education, and campus collaboration.

Donors and volunteers

Support emergency glasses, learning, community programs, and future innovation.
