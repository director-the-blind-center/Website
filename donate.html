Donate

Show your support for independence and community

Donations help The Blind Center provide education, programs, assistive technology support, resources, and a welcoming place for blind and visually impaired neighbors.

Ask About Donating

Ways to give

The Center should confirm current online donation, drop-off, receipt, and pickup details before launch.

Monetary Gifts

One-time and monthly gifts can support classes, equipment, resources, and program operations.

In-Kind Donations

The old site listed tin cans, sneakers, cars, boats, trailers, and glass recycling. Contact the Center before bringing items.

Vehicle and Large Item Donations

Cars, boats, and trailers may require special coordination, title information, and receipt handling.

Planned or Business Giving

Community partners and donors can ask about sponsorships, planned gifts, or program-specific support.

Donor information to confirm

  • Online payment processor and secure donation link
  • Drop-off hours and accepted item condition
  • Pickup options for large items
  • Tax receipt process for a North Carolina 501(c)(3)
