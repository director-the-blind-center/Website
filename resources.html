Community Resource Network

Helping you find the right resource at the right time.

Life does not organize itself by agencies. It organizes itself by challenges. The Community Resource Network uses a no-wrong-door approach.

Ask for Resource Help

I need transportation

Ask about known local transportation resources and referral options.

I need help paying for eyeglasses

Start with the Community Eyewear Center and partner options.

I need food or housing support

Ask for resource navigation and partner referrals.

I need benefits information

Ask about agencies that can help with benefits and paperwork.

I need employment support

Ask about job training, work readiness, or referral pathways.

I do not know where to begin

Call or visit and describe what is hardest today.
