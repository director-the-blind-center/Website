Programs

Learning, wellness, creativity, and technology support

The Blind Center helps people build independence, confidence, and connection through hands-on programs. Contact the Center to confirm current schedules, registration, fees, and accessibility needs.

Contact Us To Register

Program categories

Offerings may change by season, instructor availability, and community need.

Independent Living and Technology

Technology lab access, technical support, life skills, educational training, and practical tools for daily tasks.

Cooking and Home Skills

Cooking classes and independent living activities that support safer, more confident routines at home.

Health and Wellness

Exercise, yoga and meditation, healthier living workshops, and DEEP diabetes education when available.

Creative Studios

Weaving, printing, basket weaving, open craft studios, try-it workshops, and other tactile creative activities.

Community and Recreation

Social connection, group activities, seasonal events, and opportunities to participate in community life.

Work Readiness and Job Training

Training, goal planning, referrals, and support for participants building toward work or service opportunities.

Registration and accommodations

To register or ask whether a program is active, call or email the Center. Participants can request assistance completing forms, reading materials, or accessing program information in a different format.

Suggest a program

Community members, caregivers, volunteers, and partners may suggest workshops that support independence, wellness, technology access, or social connection.
