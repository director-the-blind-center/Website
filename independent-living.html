How the Center can help

Focus on one real-life task at a time.

The Center can help you think through daily routines, identify tools or techniques to try, connect with ENCight Academy classes, and explore technology or resource options that support independence.

  • Start by naming the task you want to keep doing: cooking breakfast, reading mail, managing medicine, using a phone, or staying connected.
  • Ask about large print, lighting, contrast, labels, magnification, audio, and adaptive strategies.
  • Bring a family member or supporter if helpful, but the conversation should center the person seeking independence.
