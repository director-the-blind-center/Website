What to expect

You can start with a call, email, or visit.

You do not need to know a program name before contacting The Blind Center. Share what you are trying to do, what has become difficult, or how you want to help. Staff can point you toward eyewear support, vision-change guidance, independent living resources, technology help, classes, volunteer opportunities, giving options, or a visit plan.

  • Confirm current public hours before traveling.
  • Ask ahead about accessibility, large print, verbal directions, or help with forms.
  • Bring any notes, glasses, prescriptions, devices, questions, or support person that would make the conversation easier.
